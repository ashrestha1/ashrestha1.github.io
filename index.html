This project aims to extend on pre-exisiting online communication applications through focusing directly on the needs of the hearing-impaired.

The goal is to apply the concept of design process introduced in the lectures and gain hands-on experience through this project.

Our learning and execution process

Empathize

Brainstorm

We explored prominent communication applications and tried to identify gaps in the market.

After the first round of brainstorming, we concluded that we as a group wanted to shift our attention towards users that struggle with technology in general.

Our brainstorming resulted in senior citizens as our potential target users but found out that an alarming percent of seniors suffered from some degree of hearing loss. We were concerned by the fact that there was lack of support for the deaf from communication applications, hence we ended up with shifting our target user to deaf people.

Interpret

Constructed POV

Point-Of-View plays a critical part in understanding our target users. To try and empathize with our target users we conducted a mini-experiment. We decided to mute a live lecture video and attempt to follow the lesson, however we were unable to comprehend the slides shown and lose attention in a matter of minutes.

From this experiment we deduced the needs of our target users, which will be utilised to aid our design process.
